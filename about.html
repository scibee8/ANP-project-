This is my final project for ANP 412 at Michigan State University. I would like to give credit to the Natural History Museum Vienna for the 3D models I used which I obtained from thier Sketchfab. I would also like to give credit to this article: https://medium.com/swlh/build-your-location-based-augmented-reality-web-app-a841956eed2c and it author for some of the code used to make this web app.

The models displayed are the Venus of Willendorf, a bone point fragment found at the same site as the Venus of Willendorf, and a Neolithic logboat model found at the Mondsee World Heritage Site.
